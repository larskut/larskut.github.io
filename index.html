Lars Kutschinski

PhD Candidate in Statistics

Welcome to my personal website! I am a PhD student in the department of Industrial & Systems Engineering at Korean Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) with a focus on statistical and machine learning at the. I hold a Master of Science in Statistics from the University of Toronto and a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from the University of Heidelberg.

My current research focuses on statistical transfer learning, developing methods that leverage auxiliary data to improve prediction in target tasks. My focus lies in model- and distribution-based frameworks, including high-dimensional regression and functional models, with an emphasis on transferability conditions, minimax rates, and robustness under domain shift.

I'm passionate about leveraging Statistics and Machine Learning to solve real-world problems and have experience in both academic and professional settings. My multilingual abilities (English, German, Mandarin, and French) enable me to work effectively in international environments.
